Indian Army's Bravery

Remembering the attacks and the courage of our soldiers

Pulwama Attack

The Pulwama attack was a heinous act of terrorism that occurred on 14 February 2019. A suicide bomber attacked a convoy of CRPF personnel, resulting in the loss of 40 brave soldiers. This attack was one of the deadliest on Indian forces in decades.

Surgical Strike

In response to the Uri attack, the Indian Army conducted surgical strikes on 29 September 2016. The strikes targeted militant launch pads across the Line of Control in Pakistan-administered Kashmir, showcasing the bravery and precision of the Indian Armed Forces.

Uri Attack

The Uri attack took place on 18 September 2016, when four heavily armed militants attacked an Indian Army brigade headquarters near the town of Uri in the state of Jammu and Kashmir. 19 Indian soldiers were martyred in this attack, making it one of the deadliest on Indian forces in recent years.

Galwan Valley Clash

On 15 June 2020, a violent face-off occurred between Indian and Chinese troops in the Galwan Valley, resulting in the deaths of 20 Indian soldiers. The clash marked a significant escalation in the ongoing border tensions between India and China.
