About Our Soldiers

The Indian Army, with its rich tapestry of history and valor, stands as a steadfast guardian of India's sovereignty and an embodiment of courage and sacrifice. From the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas to the deserts of Rajasthan, the Indian Army's journey is woven with tales of bravery, resilience, and unwavering commitment to duty. This tribute page celebrates the indomitable spirit of the Indian Army, honoring its legacy of service to the nation and its people.

Mission & Values

The Indian Army is guided by its mission to protect the nation's territorial integrity and uphold the sovereignty of India. The core values that define the Indian Army include:

  • Discipline: Maintaining the highest standards of discipline and professionalism.

  • Honor:Upholding the honor of the nation and the army.

  • Duty:Commitment to duty above self, ensuring the safety and security of the country.

  • Sacrifice: Recognizing and honoring the ultimate sacrifices made by soldiers for the nation.

Structure & Organisation

The Indian Army is organized into various branches and regiments, each specializing in different aspects of warfare and defense. The primary branches include:

  • Infantry:The backbone of the army, responsible for ground combat operations.

  • Artillery: Providing firepower support with advanced weaponry and missile systems.

  • Armored Corps: Equipped with tanks and armored vehicles for rapid and decisive offensive operations

  • Engineers:Equipped with tanks and armored vehicles for rapid and decisive offensive operations

Key Operations and Contributions

The Indian Army's contributions extend beyond combat. It plays a vital role in humanitarian missions and disaster relief operations, both domestically and internationally. Key operations include:

  • Peacekeeping Missions: Participating in United Nations peacekeeping missions to maintain global peace and stability.

  • Disaster Relief: Providing immediate assistance during natural disasters, such as earthquakes, floods, and cyclones.

  • Internal Security: Assisting in maintaining internal security and law and order during times of crisis
