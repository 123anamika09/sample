Indian Army's role in the Indo-Pakistani War of 1947. Independence and Partition: The Indian Army was divided between India and Pakistan. The First Indo-Pak War (1947-1948) over Kashmir.

1962

Sino-Indian War: Conflict between India and China over border disputes.

Second Indo-Pak War: Another war with Pakistan, primarily over Kashmir.

1971

Indo-Pak War: Leading to the creation of Bangladesh. Indian Army's decisive victory.

1984

Operation Blue Star: Indian Army's operation to remove militants from the Golden Temple in Amritsar.

1999

Kargil War: Conflict with Pakistan in the Kargil district of Jammu and Kashmir.

2001

Attack on Indian Parliament: Led to Operation Parakram, a military standoff with Pakistan.

Mumbai Attacks: The Indian Army's National Security Guard (NSG) played a crucial role in counter-terrorism operations.

2019

Balakot Airstrike: Airstrikes on terrorist camps in Pakistan in response to the Pulwama attack.

2020

Galwan Valley Clash: Skirmishes with Chinese troops in Eastern Ladakh.

2021-2022

Modernization and Reforms: Ongoing efforts to modernize the Indian Army with new technology and restructuring.

2023

Increased focus on cybersecurity and digital warfare capabilities.
